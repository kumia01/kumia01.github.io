Solution

Many factors contribute to the problem of attendance, two of which are well known and have been addressed extensively—the problems of access and quality. A third problem—the relevance of schooling to the lives of the children—has been largely overlooked. (Kilde, bokmerke) Our solution focuses on the third problem. We wish to give hope for a better future by showing the possibilities of education. Counting attending days and rewarding the parents who keep their children in school. The reward is mainly a future work-place promise.

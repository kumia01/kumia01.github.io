Welcome to Inova

Our purpose

The Inova Company was founded by Per Olsen and Jada Loliwe. Their main purpose is to make innovation and design accessible to all. The company is based around frugal innovation.

Our intention is to make frugal innovation become the first choice of method when it comes to development, design and entrepreneurship. Frugal innovation is about accessibility and universal design that works well for people from all walks of life and situations.

Our purpose is to share our knowledge on the subject and help emerging businesses achieve their innovation and development goals.

Our expertise

Collectively, our team holds 40 years of experience in the fields of innovation, development and business management.Together we wish to provide the best coaching on the market.

We have developed a framework that serves as a best practice guide for firms throughout the entire value creation process of a frugal innovation. The framework is step-based. We start by working out what the challenge is and how to solve it with the user in mind. we often implement methods from Lean-frameworks to solve challenges at hand.

Our camps consist of working through unique cases and how our previous clients have solved them. We take questions from audience and provide a time slot to practice the methods introduced.

What is Inova?

Inova host Innovation camps where the focus is around frugal innovation. We help to start and build businesses. Module Desks was once just a funny idea from one of the college student that took part in one of our innovation camps Now it is the most successful and profitable furniture company in Denmark with enormous potential for expansion. With a beautifully sleek and module based design it is the new favorite for startups and company’s that need desks in their offices.

The company behind out website

Web-HoBo’s is an up and coming design company out of Norway that we have partnered up with for our website. Their style of running their company is sign of what companies might look like in new future in an extremely connected world. The company have no offices and most of our employer’s do not own homes. The website name comes from the lifestyle of Web-Hobo’s founders. Eirik, Thien, Martin, Ivana and Bjørge where living in vans driving across Europe skiing down tall mountains and surfing on long beaches, when their combined interest in coding, art and design made them start their own company.
